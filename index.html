Below the Iceberg

The Power of Bridge Connections

Counterintuitive finding from 50 years of research: acquaintances open more doors than close friends when it comes to jobs.

Close friends know what you know‚Äîan echo chamber. But acquaintances move in different circles. They bring opportunities from worlds you've never accessed.

Researchers call these "consequential strangers"‚Äîpeople who bridge you to orthogonal worlds.

Privileged youth have built-in bridges: alumni networks, parents' contacts. Those at the bottom have strong family ties but lack bridges to different worlds.

Research: Strength of Weak Ties (Granovetter, 1973)
Most people find jobs through acquaintances, not close friends. Confirmed across 55 countries. Strong ties provide emotional support. Bridge connections open unexpected doors.

How Mentoring Actually Works: Harish & Akshay

Harish (45, PSU Finance Manager) mentors Akshay (22, his maid's son who dropped out after Class 12). He doesn't teach finance‚Äîhe tests accountability.

The Project

Every day, Harish sends 5 stock prices via WhatsApp. Akshay records them in Excel by 10 AM.

What's Assessed

Not finance. Accountability. Does he do it daily? Does he catch errors? Does he need reminders?

The Badges

After 3 weeks: add complexity. Each milestone = new badge. After 2 months: multiple verified skills.

The Bridge

Harish tells a friend: "I have a young man who's tracked stocks for 2 months. Never missed a day."

Result: Akshay gets a temp job at an accounting firm. Harish's badge was his credential. Harish's introduction was his bridge.

Why Now

Technology finally makes this possible

The concept of skill validation isn't new. What's new is the technology to decentralize it.

Old Model: Centralized Trust

  • Only institutions issue credentials
  • Expensive and time-consuming
  • Can't verify informal learning
  • Credentials can be faked
  • Institution owns the record
  • No relationship created

New Model: Decentralized Trust

  • Anyone who witnesses can badge
  • Free, earned through actions
  • Life experience counts
  • Tamper-proof blockchain
  • Individual owns credentials forever
  • Every badge = new connection
